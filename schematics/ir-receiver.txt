IR Receiver Circuit
====================

Components:
- CHQ1838 IR Receiver Module (38 kHz carrier)
- 10μF capacitor (optional, for decoupling)

Schematic:

    CHQ1838 front view (dome facing you):

         ┌──────────┐
         │  ┌────┐  │
         │  │ ██ │  │
         │  └────┘  │
         │          │
         │  │  │  │ │
         └──┼──┼──┼─┘
            1  2  3

    Pin 1 (OUT)  ──── GPIO14
    Pin 2 (GND)  ──── GND
    Pin 3 (VCC)  ──── 3.3V (NOT 5V!)

Optional decoupling:

    VCC ──┬── 10μF (+)
          │    capacitor
          └── 10μF (–) ──── GND

Wiring:

    CHQ1838 Pin 1 (OUT) ──► GPIO14 (IR receive data)
    CHQ1838 Pin 2 (GND) ──► GND
    CHQ1838 Pin 3 (VCC) ──► 3.3V

⚠️  WARNING: Do NOT connect CHQ1838 VCC to 5V!
    The module is rated for 3.3V only. 5V will
    permanently damage the component.

⚠️  WARNING: When using both IR LED and receiver,
    keep them physically separated (at least 5cm)
    to prevent the LED from blinding the receiver.

Operation:
  The CHQ1838 demodulates 38kHz IR signals and outputs
  the decoded digital signal on the OUT pin. The ESP32
  IRrecv library reads this signal via GPIO14 and
  decodes it into a protocol and data value.
